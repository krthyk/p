What is Under 25?

Under 25 is a dynamic platform exclusively for students, designed to help you earn, learn, and grow. It's more than just an app; it's a vibrant community where you can find opportunities, connect with like-minded peers, and build your future. Click here to know more

What's Inside

Unlock a world of possibilities with the Under 25 app. Here's a glimpse of what awaits you.

Missions

Dive into a world of interactive tasks and challenges designed to help you apply your knowledge in real-world scenarios. By completing missions, you not only earn valuable rewards and exclusive merchandise but also build a portfolio of work that demonstrates your capabilities to future employers. It's a fun, engaging way to learn by doing and get recognized for your skills. Read More

Jobs & Internships

Kickstart your career with our curated selection of jobs, internships, and freelance projects from top companies and innovative startups. Our platform is designed to match your skills and interests with the perfect opportunity, helping you gain invaluable industry experience, build your professional network, and make a real impact even before you graduate. Read More

XP Leaderboard

Gamify your growth and see where you stand among your peers. Earn Experience Points (XP) for every mission you complete, every event you attend, and every connection you make. Climb the ranks on our dynamic leaderboard, unlock achievement badges, and gain recognition for your hard work and dedication. It’s a motivating way to track your progress and celebrate your milestones. Read More

Spaces

Find your tribe by joining Spaces—dedicated groups centered around your interests, from coding and design to marketing and entrepreneurship. In these communities, you can connect with like-minded students, share ideas, ask questions, collaborate on projects, and learn from each other in a supportive and inspiring environment. It's networking, but more meaningful. Read More

Events

Never stop learning with our calendar of exclusive events. Gain access to expert-led workshops, insightful webinars with industry leaders, hackathons, and nationwide competitions. These events are designed to help you acquire new skills, stay ahead of industry trends, and expand your professional network, all from within the Under 25 ecosystem. Read More

Community

Become part of something bigger. The Under 25 Community is the heart of our platform, a thriving network of India’s brightest and most ambitious students. Here, you can forge lasting friendships, find mentors to guide your career path, discover collaborators for your next big idea, and grow together in a culture of mutual support and encouragement. Read More

How to Start

Joining our community and starting your journey is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Download the App

Visit the App Store or Google Play Store, search for "Under 25 Community," and install it on your smartphone. The app is completely free and your gateway to all our features. Click here to download the app

2. Create Your Profile

Sign up and build your profile. Highlight your skills, experiences, and interests. A complete and compelling profile will help you stand out and attract the right opportunities.

3. Explore & Engage

Start exploring! Browse through missions, apply for internships, join Spaces that match your passions, and participate in events. The more you engage, the more you earn and grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Check out our FAQ section to find what you're looking for.

What kind of opportunities can I find on the Under 25 app?

expand_more

The Under 25 app is your gateway to a wide range of opportunities, including exclusive internships, part-time jobs, freelance projects, and volunteering roles from top companies and startups. We also host various competitions and challenges to help you showcase your skills.

Is the Under 25 app free to use?

expand_more

Yes, the Under 25 app is completely free for all students to download and use. Our mission is to provide equal access to opportunities and resources to help every student succeed.

How does the community feature work?

expand_more

Our community feature allows you to connect with like-minded students from across the country. You can join interest-based groups (Spaces), participate in discussions, collaborate on projects, and find mentors to guide you on your journey. It's a great way to network and learn from your peers.

Can I use the Under 25 app to build my portfolio?

expand_more

Absolutely! By participating in freelance gigs, internships, and competitions, you can build a strong portfolio of your work. The app provides a platform to showcase your projects and skills to potential employers and collaborators.
